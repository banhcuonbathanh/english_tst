# ☀️ Weather Conversation

### Conversation 1: What's the Weather Like?

**Teacher:** Hello, Tom! How's the weather today?

**Tom:** It's sunny!

**Teacher:** Great! What can we do on a sunny day?

**Tom:** We can go to the park.

**Teacher:** Is it hot or cool?

**Tom:** It's warm and sunny.

---

### Conversation 2: Rainy Day

**Teacher:** Look outside. What's the weather like?

**Anna:** It's rainy.

**Teacher:** Can we play outside?

**Anna:** No, we can't.

**Teacher:** What can we do?

**Anna:** We can read books at home.

---

### Conversation 3: Winter Weather

**Teacher:** How is the weather in winter?

**Ben:** It's cold.

**Teacher:** Is there snow?

**Ben:** Yes! There is snowfall.

**Teacher:** What do you see?

**Ben:** I see snowflakes and ice.

---

### Conversation 4: Seasons

**Teacher:** What is your favorite season?

**Lucy:** Summer.

**Teacher:** Why?

**Lucy:** Because it is sunny and warm.

**Teacher:** What do you do in summer?

**Lucy:** I go swimming.

# 📚 Grammar for 6-Year-Olds

## 1. Asking About Weather

### Pattern

**What's the weather like?**

### Answers

* It's sunny.
* It's rainy.
* It's windy.
* It's cloudy.
* It's snowy.
* It's foggy.

### Practice

**Teacher:** What's the weather like?

**Child:** It's _______.

---

## 2. Using "It's"

### Pattern

**It's + weather word.**

Examples:

* It's sunny.
* It's hot.
* It's cold.
* It's windy.
* It's humid.

### Game

Show a picture and ask:

**What is it?**

**Answer:** It's rainy.

---

## 3. Using "I like"

### Pattern

**I like + season/weather.**

Examples:

* I like summer.
* I like winter.
* I like sunny days.
* I like rainy days.

### Practice

**Teacher:** What season do you like?

**Child:** I like spring.

---

## 4. Using "Can"

### Pattern

**We can + activity.**

Examples:

* We can swim in summer.
* We can fly a kite on a windy day.
* We can build a snowman in winter.
* We can stay inside on a stormy day.

### Practice

**Teacher:** What can you do on a snowy day?

**Child:** I can build a snowman.

---

## 5. There is / There are

### Pattern

**There is + one thing**

* There is a rainbow.
* There is frost on the grass.

**There are + many things**

* There are clouds in the sky.
* There are snowflakes falling.

### Practice

**Teacher:** What do you see?

**Child:** There is a rainbow.

---

# 🌈 Easy Weather Quiz

1. What is the weather like when the sun shines?

   * ☑ Sunny

2. What falls from clouds?

   * ☑ Rain

3. Which season is very cold?

   * ☑ Winter

4. What do you see after rain and sunshine?

   * ☑ Rainbow

5. What tool measures temperature?

   * ☑ Thermometer

**Windy, windy, blow so high,**
**Clouds are moving in the sky!**
