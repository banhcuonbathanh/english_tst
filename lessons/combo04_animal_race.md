# 🐢 Combo Lesson 4 — The Great Animal Race

**Built from Question Bank sections:** 7 (Animals & Abilities) + 20 (Nature, Space & Science) + Grammar Gym Round F (superlatives)
**Grammar focus:** **can / can't** + comparatives & superlatives ("faster than", "the fastest") + "but" ("He is fast, **but** he can't swim!")
**Spelling set:** *frog, fast, slow, win, river, snake*

## 📖 Story: *The Great Animal Race* (Cuộc đua muông thú)

> All the animals come to the Great Race. The prize: the biggest, sweetest mango in the forest!
> The **cheetah** laughs: "I am the **fastest** animal! The mango is mine!"
> The monkey says: "But I am the **best climber**!" The kangaroo says: "And I can jump the **farthest**!"
> The little **turtle** says quietly: "I want to race too." Everyone laughs: "You? You are the **slowest**!"
> READY… SET… GO! The cheetah runs faster than the wind. The kangaroo jumps higher than the trees. The turtle walks… slowly… slowly…
> But in the middle of the race, there is a big, deep **river**!
> The cheetah stops. "I can run, **but** I can't swim!" The kangaroo stops. "I can jump, **but** not THAT far!" The monkey cries: "There are no trees over the river!"
> Then — splash! The turtle slides into the water. "I am slow on land, **but** I can swim very well!"
> She swims across the river and crosses the **finish line**. All the animals **cheer**!
> The turtle shares the mango with everyone — because that's what **winners** do.

**Story questions:**
1. Who is the fastest on land? Who is the slowest?
2. Why did the cheetah stop at the river? (He can…, but he can't…)
3. Who won? Why could SHE win? (slow on land, BUT…)
4. What did the turtle do with the mango? Was she kind?
5. **Thinking question:** Is it better to be fast, or to never give up? Why?

**⭐ The big task:** he invents the NEXT race. New animals (an elephant? a bird? a snake?), a new problem (a wall? fire? ice?), and a surprise winner — and tells the story with "can / can't / but / the …est".

## 🎮 Family game: Family Animal Olympics (cả nhà cùng chơi)

Everyone draws a secret animal from a bowl (papers HE wrote: *cheetah, turtle, snail, kangaroo, frog, elephant*).

1. **Round 1 — Move like it!** Each player crosses the room AS their animal. Others guess with full questions: "Are you a snail? Are you a kangaroo?"
2. **Round 2 — The races.** Three silly events, everyone competes as their animal: *slowest walk wins* (snail race!), *longest jump* (kangaroo!), *funniest swim on the floor* (turtle!). He is the commentator: "Daddy Snail is slower than Grandma Snail! Grandma is the SLOWEST! Grandma WINS!"
3. **Round 3 — But!** Each player must say one sentence about their animal with **but**: "I am big, but I am afraid of mice!" (elephant). Best "but" sentence gets a bonus point.
4. **Medal ceremony:** he hands out paper medals: "The gold medal goes to the slowest snail in the world… GRANDMA!"

**Also fits tonight:** Who Am I? with animal papers, 20 Questions — see the [Family Game Bank](family_games.md).
