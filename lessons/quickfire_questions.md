# ⚡ Quick-Fire Question Bank (Ngân hàng câu hỏi nhanh)

Use 8–10 of these in every warm-up, and anytime during the day (car, dinner, bath).
**Rules:**
1. He must answer in a **full sentence**.
2. Level up: answer must have **two pieces of information** ("It's sunny **and** hot.").
3. Level up more: after his answer, ask a **follow-up** — *Why? / What about you? / Tell me more.*
4. Best of all: some days, **swap roles** — HE asks, YOU answer (sometimes answer wrong, so he corrects you: "No! Today is NOT Sunday, it's Tuesday!").

## 💛 13. Heart Questions — Câu hỏi tâm lý (to understand him)

**⚠️ Different rules for this section:**
- This is NOT a grammar drill. **Never correct his English here.** If he switches to Vietnamese, let him — then gently say the English version back, and move on.
- Ask only **1–2 per day**, at calm moments (bedtime is best, or walking, or in the car — not face-to-face at a desk).
- **Never judge the answer** ("Don't say that about your teacher!") — if you judge, he will stop telling you the truth.
- Answer the questions about YOURSELF too. He opens up when you open up first: "Today I felt tired because… What about you?"

| Question | Tiếng Việt (so you ask it right) | What it tells you |
|----------|----------------------------------|-------------------|
| What was the best part of your day? | Điều gì vui nhất trong ngày hôm nay của con? | What he values |
| What was the worst part of your day? | Điều gì tệ nhất trong ngày hôm nay? | Daily struggles he may not mention |
| What made you laugh today? | Hôm nay điều gì làm con cười? | His sense of humor, who he enjoys |
| Did anything make you sad or angry today? | Hôm nay có điều gì làm con buồn hay tức giận không? | Conflicts, frustrations |
| What are you afraid of? | Con sợ điều gì nhất? | Fears (dark, dogs, being alone, failing…) |
| What do you love about Mommy? | Con yêu điều gì ở mẹ? | What he needs from mom |
| What do you love about Daddy? | Con yêu điều gì ở bố? | What he needs from dad |
| Is there anything Mommy/Daddy does that you don't like? | Có điều gì bố/mẹ làm mà con không thích không? | ⭐ Hard to hear — but gold. Don't defend yourself, just say "Thank you for telling me." |
| Who is your best friend? Why do you like them? | Bạn thân nhất của con là ai? Vì sao con thích bạn ấy? | How he chooses friends |
| Did anyone make you feel bad at school? | Có ai làm con buồn ở trường không? | Early warning for bullying or conflicts |
| Is your teacher kind to you? What does she do? | Cô giáo có tốt với con không? Cô làm gì? | His relationship with the teacher |
| What are you really good at? | Con nghĩ con giỏi nhất việc gì? | Self-confidence — listen for "nothing," that matters |
| What is hard for you? | Việc gì khó với con? | Where he feels weak; never laugh at this answer |
| When do you feel proud of yourself? | Khi nào con thấy tự hào về bản thân? | What achievement means to him |
| If you had one wish, what would it be? | Nếu có một điều ước, con ước gì? | Deep wants (a toy = normal; "more time with daddy" = listen carefully) |
| When you are sad, what helps you feel better? | Khi buồn, điều gì giúp con thấy vui hơn? | How to comfort him — use his own answer next time he cries |
| Do you like it when people watch you do things, or do you like to do them alone? | Con thích mọi người xem con làm, hay thích làm một mình? | Personality: performer vs. private |
| Would you rather play with many friends or one friend? | Con thích chơi với nhiều bạn hay một bạn thôi? | Introvert / extrovert tendencies |
| What do you want me to do more with you? | Con muốn bố/mẹ chơi gì với con nhiều hơn? | His real request to YOU — try to do it this week |
| What should we do when you get angry? | Khi con tức giận, bố mẹ nên làm gì? | Lets HIM design the calm-down plan — he'll follow his own plan better than yours |

**📝 Keep a small note in your phone:** date + question + his answer. After a month you will see patterns — what he repeats is what matters most to him. Answers change as he grows; ask the favorites again every few months.

## 📈 How to use the bank week by week

- **Weeks 1–4:** sections 1, 2, 3, 4 — answers with two details. Spelling Level 1 daily.
- **Weeks 5–8:** add 5, 6, 9 (School), 11 (Night), 15 (Body), 16 (Clothes) + always one *Why?* follow-up. Grammar Gym Round A (past tense) twice a week. Spelling Level 1–2.
- **Weeks 9–12:** add 7, 10 (Sports), 12 (Love/Hate), 8, 17 (Town), 18 (Transport), 21 (Numbers) + he asks YOU 3 questions every warm-up. Grammar Gym Rounds A, C, E. Word Builder: Opposites + Category Race. Spelling Level 2 + word families.
- **Weeks 13–16:** add 19 (Jobs), 20 (Space & Science). Everything, including past and future + Correct Me! every session. Grammar Gym all rounds (B "Have you ever" and F "the MOST!" are the boss level). Word Builder: Riddles + Odd One Out. Spelling Level 3 + spelling games.
- **Section 13 (Heart Questions) is separate** — not for lessons. Use it at bedtime, 1–2 questions a day, any week, with its own gentle rules.

**Daily shape (1 hour):** 10 min quick-fire warm-up (8–10 questions, mixed sections) → 5 min one Grammar Gym round → main lesson → 5 min one Word Builder game → 5 min Spelling Power to finish.

**Speed round (he loves to win):** set a 2-minute timer — how many questions can he answer in full sentences? Write the score down, beat it next time.
