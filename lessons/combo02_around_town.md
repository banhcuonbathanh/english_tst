# 🏙️ Combo Lesson 2 — Around Our Town

**Built from Question Bank sections:** 17 (My Town & Directions) + 18 (Transport) + 19 (Jobs)
**Grammar focus:** "There is / There are" + directions (**go straight, turn left, turn right, it's next to…**) + "A ___ works in a ___."
**Spelling set:** *shop, park, bus, train, left, right*

## 📖 Story: *The Lost Puppy* (Chú cún đi lạc)

> A little puppy is **lost** in the big city. "Where is my home?" he cries.
> First, he goes to the **bakery**. The **baker** says: "I bake bread, I don't know your home. But go straight and ask at the pharmacy!"
> At the **pharmacy**, the pharmacist says: "I give people medicine. Turn left and ask at the school!"
> At the **school**, the **teacher** says: "I teach children! Hmm… A boy in my class lost a puppy! He lives next to the park. Turn right at the corner, go straight, and you will see a yellow house!"
> The puppy runs — turn right, go straight — and there it is: the yellow house, next to the park!
> The boy opens the door. "LUCKY! You came home!" Lucky jumps and barks: "Woof! Woof!"
> That night, Lucky sleeps in his warm bed. Being home is the best.

**Story questions:**
1. Who works at the bakery? What does he do?
2. What did the pharmacist say? (Turn… and ask at the…)
3. Where does the boy live? (next to the…, in the… house)
4. What should YOU do if you are lost? (ask a police officer, say my address)

**⭐ The big task:** he retells the puppy's journey while drawing the map: "First the puppy went to the bakery. The baker said go straight…" — then HE adds one more stop to the story (a restaurant? a fire station?) with a new worker.

## ⏰ 1-Hour Plan

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 0–8 min | Warm-up: 10 quick-fire questions from sections **17, 18, 19** ("Where do we buy bread?", "Who helps sick people?", "How many wheels does a motorbike have?") |
| 8–18 min | New vocabulary: say → spell → his own sentence |
| 18–30 min | **Story:** read *The Lost Puppy* twice, draw the town map while reading, story questions |
| 30–40 min | Retell + invent one new stop for the puppy |
| 40–45 min | Writing: 3 lines — "There is a ___ on my street. There are ___. My address is ___." (Real safety bonus: he learns to SAY his real address in English.) |
| 45–55 min | 🎮 Family game: **Our House Is a Town** (below) |
| 55–60 min | He gives YOU directions to his room with eyes-closed-walking. Sticker ⭐ |

## 🎮 Family game: Our House Is a Town (cả nhà cùng chơi)

Put paper signs on rooms: kitchen = **Restaurant**, bathroom = **Pharmacy**, sofa = **Park**, parents' room = **Hotel**, his room = **Police Station**.

1. **Round 1 — Taxi!** One player is the taxi driver (walks with hands on an invisible wheel), one is the passenger: "Take me to the pharmacy, please!" The OTHER family members give directions: "Go straight! Turn left at the park!" Wrong direction = crash, everyone falls over dramatically.
2. **Round 2 — Who works here?** Stand in a "place" — others must say the job: in the Restaurant → "A chef works here! He cooks!"
3. **Round 3 — Lost Grandma!** One player (best: Grandma) is "lost" like the puppy. She walks around saying "Where is the park?? I'm lost!" and HE must rescue her with English directions to the sofa.

**Also great tonight:** Treasure Hunt with direction clues — see the [Family Game Bank](family_games.md).

## ✅ Success check
1. Gives 3-step directions (go straight, turn left, it's next to…) that actually work in the house.
2. Says 5 job sentences: "A ___ works in a ___. He/She ___s."
3. Says his real address in English.
4. Spells: *shop, park, bus, train, left, right*.
