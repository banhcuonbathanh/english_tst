Games for 3+ players вҖ” mom, dad, grandma, siblings, everyone. No cards, no printing: paper, pen, and the house.

**House rules for every game:**
1. English only during the game (grandma can whisper-ask for help вҖ” HE translates for her, that's the best practice of all).
2. Full sentences win points. One-word answers = half a point.
3. He can be the **Game Master** sometimes вҖ” the one who asks, scores, and says "Correct!" / "Wrong!" (being the boss is the strongest motivation there is).
4. Keep a score sheet on the fridge. Champions get a crown drawn next to their name.
