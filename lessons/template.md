## ⏰ 1-Hour Session Plan

### 0–5 min · 🎵 Warm-up (English only!)
8–10 quick-fire questions from the [Question Bank](quickfire_questions.md): day, weather, feelings + 2 from last week's theme. Two details per answer + one *Why?* follow-up.

### 5–12 min · 🗣️ Review + Spelling (no cards)
Last week's words: say them in Vietnamese or point at objects — he says the English word → spells it → makes a sentence. Words from the "tricky words" cup come back until he spells them right twice.

### 12–25 min · ⭐ New vocabulary
Each word: say → spell → HIS own sentence.

### 25–37 min · 📚 Grammar A
Teach the pattern, then he makes 5 original sentences (his own ideas, not repeats).

### 37–43 min · 📖 Reading — phonics sound
4–6 sentences using this week's sound, written big. He reads aloud; point out the sound.

2. ...
3. ...
4. ...

### 43–48 min · ✏️ Writing
Notebook, date in English on top. Copy 4 words + write 1 sentence from memory (dictation).

### 48–56 min · 🎮 Game — Grammar B
A question game from the Game Bank in [program.md](../program.md) (20 Questions, Detective, Interview the Toy, Wrong Sentence!…). He must ASK, not just answer.

### 56–60 min · 🗨️ Free talk + Wrap-up
He asks YOU 3 questions. Sticker ⭐, goodbye.

---

## 🏠 During the week (5 minutes a day)
- One asking task (he interviews a family member).
- One mini-diary entry (2 lines).
- Spelling challenges anywhere (car, dinner).

## ✅ Success check
1. Says + spells the new words, each in his own sentence.
2. Makes 5 original Grammar A sentences.
3. Asks the Grammar B questions without prompting.
4. Reads the phonics sentences aloud; writes 1 sentence from memory.
